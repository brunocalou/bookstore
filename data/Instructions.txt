In order to import the files, follow the tutorial:

1 - Go to your mongodb bin location
2 - mongoimport --db bookdb --collection books --file <your Books.json location> --jsonArray --drop
3 - Check it with
	mongo
	use bookdb
	db.books.find().pretty()
